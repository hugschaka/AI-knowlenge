Marketing Videos
Seedance 2.0 + HyperFrames + /goal — from prompt to campaign
Seedance 2.0 — Core Engine
Seedance 2.0 ByteDance
Quad-modal: text + image + video + audio simultaneously. Generates up to 2K with native audio in under a minute.
Basic structure
Subject + Action + Environment + Camera + Style + Constraints. 60–100 words ideal.
Golden rule
First 20–30 words decide everything. Open with who is in frame and what they're doing.
One camera move
push-in / pull-out / pan / tracking / orbit / aerial / handheld / fixed. One only.
Negative prompts (required)
avoid jitter + avoid bent limbs + avoid temporal flicker. Characters: avoid identity drift.
